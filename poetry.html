🪶 Poetry

Words that linger

Feelings I couldn't hold in — so I let them spill into words. Raw, honest, and from somewhere deep.

01
"

Never Fade

On feelings that outlast everything
There is indeed no reason to be a fire You can be water and softer.
Remember everything fades away. Everything. The mountain, the river and anger… The fire.
But the feelings never fades The feelings when our eyes meet, Which our clumsy heart can't understand.
That feelings is what we live for, Forever And we never fade….
~Amlan Feelings

"

The Mirror

On masks and innocence
We confront ourselves every morning, right in front of the mirror, don't we?
Don't we wear that mask?
Mask of an innocent person who has been judged by everyone.
Victim card. Don't we play the victim card everyday?
Okay.
I heard it. I heard it clear.
Because,
because I am also that innocent person.
~Amlan Identity

"

Far-Flung

On distance and what we had
While struggling between staying & letting go,
I realised I love so easily & break so easily
Far away from here when I was in your arms I was not so fragile;
Do we really have to be this far-flung, When we used to sit in quiet
and listen to the secrets of Rain and look at the stars Wildflowers used to bloom. We used to pluck Tahoka Daisy, And got lost in horizon….
~Amlan Longing
04
"

At the Butterflies

On love, freedom, and letting go
Dew drops on your lips, your silky eyelashes, your lustful eyes, Sunbeam on your forehead, when I hug you the aroma of your body, Your touch so blue, your love so white Lemon, paprika, sea salt.
I always wanted to be indulged in you and longed to earn a marble bust in your personal Walhalla
Sorry but I have become a nomad. A bee A kite A traveller of time, where horizon is infinity They are so limitless, so contagious
Don't wait for me at the windows, don't wait at the corner of the fence of that garden, don't wear that blue dress, don't read those old poems, don't lean on those walls.
We will meet at the butterflies.
~Amlan Freedom
05
"

Pink Flower Stories

On blooming and the uncertain in-between
Let them bloom Let Lilies bloom, let begonias bloom, let tulips bloom.
Let all of them bloom.
All of our lives are not stories, because few of them are red, few are peach and rest all who are most uncertain are pink 🌸.
Then let's meet at sun set and write the pink flower stories.
~Amlan Hope
06
"

At the Cliff

On love, nature, and timeless beauty
Let's meet at the cliff Let's sit together on the limestones Let me look at you for hours with the salty breeze on your face, and treasure your beauty. Let me write and recite poems for you.
We immerse ourselves at the coastline, the ferries coming & going, the majestic landscape, the tides hitting the cliff.
See the falcons, the hawks, the seagulls and adore the beauty, where the past and the present coexist.
We walk down amid the wildflowers to the sea, catch waves, Talk through life, laugh, hug amidst the serene reflection of nature.
~Amlan Nature
07
"

Scars

On what stays with us
Scars are the ones who stay with you always. Because they endure chaos, They endure silence.
One day they become you. And you start to wear your own scars.
~Amlan Resilience
